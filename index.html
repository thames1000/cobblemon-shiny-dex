All 70 Cobblemon berries and how to get each. Wild berries grow on trees that spawn in their listed biome(s) — harvest by right-clicking (no need to break the tree). Plant in the preferred biome or apply the preferred mulch to a stage-0 tree for bonus yield. Mutation berries can't be found: plant the two parent trees orthogonally adjacent (not diagonal) — each harvest has a 12.5% chance to drop the mutation (raise it with Surprise Mulch).
